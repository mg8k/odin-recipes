Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a delious Butter chicken recipe it is juicy flavourful.

The recipe will include chicken spices and white rice combined with heavy cream to make a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Spice the Chicken.
  2. Cook the Chicken.
  3. Boil the rice in a pot.
  4. Pour cream on chicken.
  5. Pour chicken on the rice.
