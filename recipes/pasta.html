Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a simple and delicious pasta recipe.

it will have chicken veggies like bell peppers and mushrooms. it will be creamy and yummy

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • chicken
  • bell peppers
  • mushrooms
  • cream

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Cook the chicken and vegetables in a pan until tender.
  3. Add the cream and stir until well combined.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the creamy sauce.
